## Introduction
Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) are collective organizations formed by farmers to enhance their bargaining power, improve their income, and access better services.

## Background and Definition
FPOs are defined as a group of farmers who come together to enhance their production efficiency and market access.

## Objectives
- To empower farmers with collective bargaining
- To facilitate access to markets and finance
